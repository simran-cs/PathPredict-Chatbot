\## System Architecture



PathPredict has a straightforward, conceptual system architecture designed to be clear and instructional.



The system consists of three primary components:



\- \*\*Input Layer\*\*  

&nbsp; Collects conceptual genetic risk indicators and lifestyle-related information provided by the user.



\- \*\*Processing Layer\*\*  

&nbsp; Analyzes lifestyle patterns and genetic risk concepts using AI-based reasoning, without relying on real patient data.



\- \*\*Output Layer\*\*  

&nbsp; Generates a simplified risk summary and preventive health awareness guidance in an easy-to-understand format.
